Lichess Rankings – Information

Most recent tournament: Oct 12, 2020. To navigate the rankings, use the icons above. The icon  refers to global rankings for all categories combined. The duplicate icons for bullet and blitz are used to distinguish between (hyper)bullet and (super)blitz arenas.

The rankings on this webpage are based on all regularly-scheduled arenas played on lichess.org (hourly, daily, weekly, monthly, yearly, eastern, elite, and shield arenas) as well as the seasonal 24h marathons and the titled arenas. These rankings exclude irregular rating-restricted events (<1500, <1700, ...), irregular themed arenas (King's Gambit Arena, ...), and arenas created by lichess users. In total the rankings on this webpage cover 277K events, which had a total of 1.1M unique players participating in the events.
